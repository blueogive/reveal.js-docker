##### Instructions * Whiteboard: `b` to enable/disable the whiteboard. * Annotator: `c` to enable/disable drawing on the slide * Left click (or touch and drag) to write notes on the canvas * Right click (or touch and hold, the drag) to activate the eraser * `Del` or `Bksp` to clear the canvas * `d` key to download drawing * `x` key to cycle colors forward * `y` key to cycle colors backward
